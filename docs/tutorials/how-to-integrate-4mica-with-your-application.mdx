# How to Integrate 4Mica with Your Application

A good 4Mica integration starts small. Pick one paid workflow, decide whether your application is paying, receiving, or both, and wire that path end to end before expanding.

## Integration Map

- Buyer apps need a signer, collateral, network registration, and an x402-capable HTTP client.
- Seller apps need protected routes, prices, a payTo address, guarantee verification, and settlement handling.
- Full marketplaces often need both roles: agents pay for upstream work while the app charges downstream consumers.

## Recommended Rollout

1. Register one test wallet and deposit collateral on Base Sepolia.
2. Protect one low-risk route or wrap one outbound client.
3. Log every 402 response, guarantee, verify result, and settle result.
4. Add policy controls such as seller allowlists, max price per request, and budget per task.
5. Move to production networks only after settlement monitoring is routine.

## Product Story

Use 4Mica where account setup, prepaid balances, or per-request chain transactions would slow down the user. The application should tell a simple story: connect collateral once, let agents transact instantly, and settle with clear guarantees later.

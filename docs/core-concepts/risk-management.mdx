# Risk Management

Risk management in 4Mica is about bounding exposure between the instant user experience and delayed settlement.

## Why It Matters

4Mica is the clearing house for the agentic economy: a credit layer for x402 that lets agents and applications transact instantly, earn yield on collateral, and settle net positions later.

## How It Works

- Collateral limits how much credit a wallet can extend.
- Signed guarantees scope each obligation by payer, recipient, asset, version, and request.
- SLAs and validation policies can gate payment quality.
- Locked collateral covers eligible defaults after the finality deadline.

## Builder Guidance

Start with the simplest x402 flow that works, then add stronger policies, limits, validation, and monitoring as your payment surface grows. Keep buyer and seller responsibilities explicit so failures are easy to debug.

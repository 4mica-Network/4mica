# Earning Yield

4Mica is built so idle collateral can remain productive instead of sitting as dead prepaid balance. Stablecoin deposits can route through yield-bearing infrastructure such as Aave where configured.

## Why It Matters

4Mica is the clearing house for the agentic economy: a credit layer for x402 that lets agents and applications transact instantly, earn yield on collateral, and settle net positions later.

## How It Works

- Yield accrues while collateral backs credit.
- The payer keeps the economic benefit of deposited collateral.
- Yield can offset payment costs over time.
- Risk parameters should reflect asset, protocol, and market assumptions.

## Builder Guidance

Start with the simplest x402 flow that works, then add stronger policies, limits, validation, and monitoring as your payment surface grows. Keep buyer and seller responsibilities explicit so failures are easy to debug.

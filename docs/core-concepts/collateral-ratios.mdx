# Collateral Ratios

Collateral ratios define how much credit can be extended against deposited assets. Conservative ratios protect recipients; more aggressive ratios improve capital efficiency.

## Why It Matters

4Mica is the clearing house for the agentic economy: a credit layer for x402 that lets agents and applications transact instantly, earn yield on collateral, and settle net positions later.

## How It Works

- Start conservative for new assets and new agents.
- Increase limits as counterparties and behavior become predictable.
- Consider volatility, liquidity, and claim timing.
- Expose limits clearly to agents before they start spending.

## Builder Guidance

Start with the simplest x402 flow that works, then add stronger policies, limits, validation, and monitoring as your payment surface grows. Keep buyer and seller responsibilities explicit so failures are easy to debug.

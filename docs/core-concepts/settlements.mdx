# Settlements

Settlement is the moment a signed off-chain obligation becomes an accounted payment or an enforceable claim against collateral.

## Why It Matters

4Mica is the clearing house for the agentic economy: a credit layer for x402 that lets agents and applications transact instantly, earn yield on collateral, and settle net positions later.

## How It Works

- Payable guarantees enter a clearing cycle.
- The cycle computes net debtor and creditor positions.
- Net debtors pay their positions during the payment window.
- Locked collateral covers uncovered positions after the finality deadline.
- Core services track guarantee and cycle state through settlement.

## Builder Guidance

Start with the simplest x402 flow that works, then add stronger policies, limits, validation, and monitoring as your payment surface grows. Keep buyer and seller responsibilities explicit so failures are easy to debug.

# Deposits and Withdrawals

Depositing collateral is the registration step. Withdrawing uses a timelock so the protocol can account for open tabs and avoid race conditions.

## Why It Matters

4Mica is the clearing house for the agentic economy: a credit layer for x402 that lets agents and applications transact instantly, earn yield on collateral, and settle net positions later.

## How It Works

- Deposit ETH directly or approve and deposit an ERC-20.
- Wait for finalization before relying on the balance.
- Request withdrawal when funds should leave the credit pool.
- Finalize after the configured withdrawal grace period if no obligations block it.

## Builder Guidance

Start with the simplest x402 flow that works, then add stronger policies, limits, validation, and monitoring as your payment surface grows. Keep buyer and seller responsibilities explicit so failures are easy to debug.

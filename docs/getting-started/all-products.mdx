4Mica is not a single checkout widget. It is a credit layer for x402 made of protocol contracts, a facilitator, SDKs, middleware, and hosted tools that work together.

Use this page as a map. Start with the piece closest to what you are building, then follow the linked guide when you are ready to wire it into your app.

### Protocol

The protocol layer secures the payment flow. A payer deposits collateral once, signs payment guarantees as requests happen, and settles later. If a guarantee matures and remains unpaid, the seller can claim from collateral during the claim window.
